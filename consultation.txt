# GENESIS+ Counseling Flow: Design System Documentation

## 1. Core Philosophy: "The Trust Journey"
The Counseling Flow is designed to transition the user from a state of seeking help to a state of active, secure communication. The visual language balances **Clinical Trust** (Deep Purple) with **Emotional Safety** (Classic Lilac and whitespace).

## 2. Visual Identity & Color Psychology
- **Primary (Deep Purple - #5B21B6):** Used for primary conversion points (booking buttons, "Simpan") and active branding elements. Represents stability and professional expertise.
- **Secondary (Classic Lilac - #D8B4FE):** Used for progress indicators, secondary highlights, and user-centric features. Represents empathy and emotional openness.
- **Accent (Soft Pastel Lilac - #F3E8FF):** Applied to user chat bubbles and subtle section backgrounds to provide a "soft" feel.
- **Neutral (Off-White - #F8F9FF):** The primary background color, chosen to reduce cognitive load and create a "breathable" sanctuary.

## 3. Typography (Plus Jakarta Sans & Inter)
- **Headings (H1/H2):** Plus Jakarta Sans (Bold/Semi-Bold). Used for section titles like "Temukan Teman Berbagi" to establish a warm yet authoritative tone.
- **UI Labels & Buttons:** Plus Jakarta Sans (Medium). High legibility for critical actions.
- **Body & Chat Content:** Inter (Regular). Used for message bubbles and descriptive text where maximum readability is required.

## 4. The 3-Step Interaction Framework
To minimize user overwhelm, the counseling experience is divided into three distinct, reactive states:

### Phase 1: The Trust Directory (Counseling Center)
- **Anatomy:** A responsive grid of psychologist profile cards.
- **Profile Cards:** Feature `16px` (2xl) rounded corners, subtle purple-tinted shadows, and clear hierarchy between name, credentials, and specialty tags.

### Phase 2: The Scheduling Form & Sanctuary Paper
- **Layout:** A centered, white container (`rounded-2xl`) that houses both administrative and reflective tasks.
- **Progress Indicator:** A minimalist, thin-line indicator positioned at the top of the form container.
- **Scheduling Inputs:** Standardized `8px` (lg) rounded input fields with soft border colors.
- **Sanctuary Paper:** A "Notion-style" blank canvas for the user's background story. No rigid borders; uses generous line-height (1.6) and empathetic placeholder text.

### Phase 3: Secure Counseling Chat
- **Encryption Cues:** A persistent "End-to-End Encrypted" banner at the top of the chat thread.
- **Bubble Logic:**
  - **User:** Soft Pastel Lilac background, right-aligned.
  - **Psychologist:** Pure White background with a thin gray border, left-aligned.
- **Input Area:** A floating, minimalist bar with quick-action icons (attachment, emoji) and a prominent "Send" button.

## 5. Component Language (Soft-Edge Design)
- **Border Radius:**
  - Containers/Modals: `16px` (2xl)
  - Interactive/Inputs: `8px` (lg)
- **Shadow Treatment:** `shadow-[0_4px_6px_-1px_rgba(91,33,182,0.05)]` — adding depth without creating harshness.
- **Micro-interactions:** 
  - `active:scale-95` on all primary buttons.
  - Smooth opacity transitions when switching between flow states.

## 6. Accessibility & Security Visuals
- **Safety Indicators:** Consistent use of the "Shield" and "Lock" icons in Deep Purple to reassure users of privacy.
- **Contrast:** Maintains WCAG AAA standards for all primary text content against white/off-white backgrounds.
